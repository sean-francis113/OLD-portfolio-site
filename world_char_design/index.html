The World of Hunted

"Magic has been in the world since its birth. It is a science, a part of biology and anatomy as blood and sinew. As society has evolved into the modern world new laws have been set into place. Yet, there are those that are fighting against the new system. Now, the Inquisition and Black Cults fight for supremacy while citizens fear for the future."

David Parker

"A child born without magic, David was built on the horrible atrocities the Black Cults committed. He was suspected of being a mage and was kicked out of his home. Now he is a sniper for the Inquisition who uses artifices and has many missions under his belt. Yet memories of his foster family and their deaths still haunt him."

Leah Kaitlyn

"An orphan born within an Irish church, a mage with gifts she was encouraged to leave behind and a woman who is hated by many just for who she is. Leah is a mage who taught herself how to use her gift. The Inquisition of Ireland sought her out when she saved her fellow orphans from being kidnapped. She specializes in middle to close combat and prefers her magic to any other weaponry."
