About

Sean Francis is a Writer and Game Designer who currently studies at George Mason University. He focuses on writing stories, designing worlds and characters but also has skills in other forms of game design including programming, sound design and level design. Things he does in his free time includes playing video games and reading fantasy books, his favorite being the Dresden Files series by Jim Butcher.


If you wish to read more from him, visit his Story a Week site where you can find many more stories. You can also visit Rivalcast Media where you can find The Fireside Tales which has chapters published every other week.
